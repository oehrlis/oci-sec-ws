## Topics

In this workshop, we will cover a range of key security topics related to Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI). Participants will gain practical insights and hands-on experience in areas such as Cloud Guard, Data Safe, and Security Zones. We will also touch upon other critical aspects like in-transit encryption, shielded instances, and key management. By the end of the workshop, you will have a solid foundation in OCI security best practices and tools.
